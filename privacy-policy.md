# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how this application (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App.

## Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

* Account information (such as email address or username)
* Usage data and analytics
* Device information
* Purchase and subscription information
* Customer support communications

## Subscriptions and Payments

The App may offer subscriptions or in-app purchases through Apple’s App Store.

We use [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com) to manage subscriptions and purchase status. RevenueCat may receive information related to purchases, such as:

* App User ID
* Subscription status
* Transaction and receipt information
* Device identifiers

RevenueCat processes data in accordance with its own privacy practices.

Payments are processed by Apple. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.

## Analytics

The App may use third-party analytics services to help improve functionality, diagnose crashes, and understand usage patterns.

These services may collect:

* Device type
* Operating system version
* App interactions
* Crash logs
* Approximate location derived from IP address

## How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

* Provide and maintain the App

* Respond to support requests

* Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information.

These providers may access information only as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

## Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill legal obligations.
